QUESTIONS

Before you ask

Is IqraFlow free?
Yes. The full mushaf, tajweed colouring, word-by-word translation, streaks and khatms are free. We may add optional extras later, but the core reading experience will stay free.
Does it really work offline?
Completely. The entire Quran text, translations and your progress live on your device. Recitation audio can be downloaded per surah or reciter over Wi-Fi, then played anywhere - flights, basements, anywhere.
What is a grace token?
Life happens. Miss a day and a grace token is spent automatically to protect your streak - you earn them back by reading consistently. It keeps the habit merciful instead of punishing.
How do shared khatms work?
Create a khatm, share its short invite code, and each person claims a portion - by juz or surah. Progress syncs live for the group while personal reading stays on your device. No social feed, no follower counts.
Which script, translations and reciters are included?
The Uthmani Hafs script with colour-coded tajweed, Saheeh International English translation with word-by-word meanings at launch, and 16 renowned reciters including Mishary Alafasy, Abdul Basit and Al-Husary.
When does it launch, and on what?
iOS, Android and a web app are all in the works - the apps are in store review preparation now. Join the list above and you’ll get exactly one email on launch day.
