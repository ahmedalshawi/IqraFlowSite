Privacy policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026
The short version: IqraFlow is offline-first. Your reading, streaks and stats live on your device. We don't run ads, we don't sell data, and we only sync the minimum needed to make shared khatms work.

1. What stays on your device

Everything about your personal practice: reading position and history, daily targets, streaks and grace tokens, hasanat counts, bookmarks, favourites, notes, khatm progress, and settings. This data is stored locally and never leaves your device unless you join a shared khatm or enable a backup.

2. Shared khatms

Shared khatms need a small amount of synced data: the khatm's name, your chosen display name, your claimed portion, and your progress within it (verse counts — never the content of your notes). This is visible only to people who have the khatm's invite code. Leave a khatm and your entry is removed from the group.

3. No accounts required

You can use every core feature without creating an account. Joining a shared khatm generates an anonymous identifier on your device; it is not linked to your name, email, or phone number.

4. Audio downloads

Recitation audio is fetched from our content delivery network when you download a surah or reciter. These requests include standard technical information (IP address, device type) used only to serve the files; we do not build profiles from them.

5. Notifications

Reading reminders are scheduled locally on your device. We don't send marketing push notifications.

6. Analytics & tracking

No third-party advertising SDKs, no cross-app tracking, no selling of data — ever. If we add crash reporting or anonymous usage metrics in the future, they will be privacy-preserving, aggregated, and disclosed here first.

7. The launch waiting list

If you join the waiting list on this site, we store your email address for one purpose: telling you when IqraFlow launches. You can unsubscribe with one click, and we delete the list after launch.

8. Children

IqraFlow is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data by default, children can use the app safely. Shared khatm display names are chosen freely — we recommend families choose them together.

9. Your rights & contact

You can delete all local data by deleting the app, and remove any synced khatm data from inside the app. For anything else — questions, deletion requests, concerns — email privacy@iqraflow.app. We'll reply within 30 days.

10. Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and note material changes in the app's release notes before they take effect.
